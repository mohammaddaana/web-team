Workshops & Projects

Kraken Team — Workshops, Projects & Innovation

Software Development Life Cycle

We held a specialized seminar on the Software Development Life Cycle, where we focused on transferring best practices and global standards in building software systems to our fellow students, to ensure the quality and efficiency of emerging technical projects.

Arduino technologies.

Believing that the future lies in the integration of software and hardware, we organized an intensive training course in Arduino technologies. Through this course, we enabled participants to build smart prototypes and connect the code to the real world.

Department Day

We took on the responsibility of organizing the technical career day, acting as a link between talented students and leading companies. We successfully created a professional environment that opened doors to opportunities and training for team members and the university community.

Programmer's Mindset

We believe that if there is what is more important than learning programming and programming languages, it is to learn how I should think as a programmer and analyze problems and ideas, and this workshop specializes in teaching you that.

About Kraken

We are Kraken.. Empowering the Next Generation of Tech Creators.Kraken is a premier youth-led technology team based in Jordan, dedicated to fostering innovation and technical excellence. We represent a dynamic ecosystem designed to bridge the gap between learning and industry-standard practices.Our Core Belief: We strive to ensure that Arab youth are not merely consumers or observers of technology, but active contributors and developers of its future.Through our specialized units in Web Development, Cyber Security, Game Design, AI we equip young talents with the tools to lead. From organizing national tech events to participating in international conferences, Kraken stands as a beacon of creativity and technical prowess in the regional tech scene
